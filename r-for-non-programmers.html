1 R-for-Non-Programmers

Welcome to R for Non-Programmers: A Guide for Social Scientists :wave:

This book covers all the basics of R and the fundamentals of statistical computing as they pertain to Social Sciences and related fields. Enjoy reading the book and make sure you also install the R package r4np which is the official companion for this book. It contains all datasets used and some useful functions to get you up and running even faster.
